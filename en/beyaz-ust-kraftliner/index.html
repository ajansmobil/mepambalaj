White Top Kraftliner

Hatem Paper white top kraftliner is a paper type with durable structure specially designed for the packaging industry, produced with high whiteness properties. It offers ideal solutions for packaging applications with its high whiteness and durable structure features.

Our white top kraftliner paper provides excellent visual quality thanks to its high whiteness while delivering professional results with its durable structure. With these features, it provides perfect results especially for packaging, box and applications requiring high visual quality.
