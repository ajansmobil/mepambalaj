Sack Kraft Paper

MEP Ambalaj sack kraft paper is a paper type specially designed for sack production, produced with high durability and strong structure properties. It offers ideal solutions for sack production with its high durability and strong structure features.

Our sack kraft paper provides excellent durability thanks to its special kraft technology while delivering professional results with its strong structure. With these features, it provides perfect results especially for sacks, bags and applications requiring durability.

With our sustainable production approach, we provide environmentally friendly sack kraft paper solutions and supply the most suitable products for our customers' sack needs.
