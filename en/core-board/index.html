Core Board

Hatem Paper core board is a cardboard type with high durability specially designed for packaging applications, produced with core cardboard technology. It offers ideal solutions for packaging applications with its high durability and special design features.

Our core board cardboard provides excellent durability thanks to its core structure while delivering professional results with its high quality. With these features, it provides perfect results especially for packaging, box and applications requiring high durability.

With our sustainable production approach, we provide environmentally friendly core board solutions and supply the most suitable products for our customers' packaging needs.
