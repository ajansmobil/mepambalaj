Newsprint

As Hatem Paper, we are a pioneer in the industry with our 35 years of experience in high-quality newsprint production. Our premium quality newsprint, sourced from our European and Far Eastern suppliers, is produced with low cost and environmentally friendly production standards.

Our newsprint offers ideal solutions for professional printing applications with its high opacity, excellent print quality and durable structure. Thanks to its low grammage, it is cost-effective and increases visual quality with its high whiteness.

With our sustainable production approach, we provide environmentally friendly newsprint solutions and supply the most suitable products for our customers' needs.
