Improved Newsprint

Hatem Paper's improved newsprint offers superior features compared to traditional newsprint. It is an ideal solution for professional printing applications with better print quality, improved opacity and low cost advantages.

This specially designed paper provides perfect results for magazine and catalog printing with high whiteness, excellent surface smoothness and improved printing performance. It offers sustainable solutions with environmentally friendly production processes.
