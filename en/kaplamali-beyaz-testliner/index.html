Coated White Testliner

Hatem Paper coated white testliner is a paper type specially designed for packaging applications, produced with high quality and excellent print surface properties. It offers ideal solutions for packaging applications with its high quality and excellent print surface features.

Our coated white testliner paper provides excellent print quality thanks to its special coating technology while delivering professional results with its high whiteness. With these features, it provides perfect results especially for packaging, box and applications requiring high print quality.

With our sustainable production approach, we provide environmentally friendly coated white testliner paper solutions and supply the most suitable products for our customers' packaging needs.
