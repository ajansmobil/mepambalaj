User Rights

Everyone has the right to request the protection of their personal data. The protection of your personal data is very important to us and we act in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK) No. 6698 and relevant legislation. Users have the right to be informed about for what purposes their data is collected, how it is stored and with whom it is shared.

You can request to update, correct, delete and anonymize your personal data. At the same time, you can request detailed information about for what purpose the collected data is used and the processing process, and get information about third parties involved in these processes. You can always contact us to exercise your rights.

You can review the Privacy Policy on our website to get detailed information about the protection of your personal data and your rights. As a user, the security of your data is our priority and we have taken the necessary technical and administrative measures.
