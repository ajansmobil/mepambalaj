LWC Paper

Hatem Paper LWC paper is a high-quality paper type produced with light weight coated technology. It offers special design solutions for magazine and advertising printing with its low grammage and high quality.

Our LWC paper provides excellent print quality thanks to its thin coating layer while offering cost-effective solutions with its low grammage. With these features, it is ideal especially for magazine and catalog printing.

Our LWC paper, which offers sustainable solutions with environmentally friendly production processes, provides perfect results for professional printing applications.
