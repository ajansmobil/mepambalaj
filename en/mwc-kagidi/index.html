MWC Paper

Hatem Paper MWC paper is a high-quality paper type produced with medium weight coated technology. It offers ideal solutions for professional printing applications with its versatile use feature.

Our MWC paper provides excellent print quality thanks to its balanced coating thickness while offering cost-effective solutions with its medium grammage. With these features, it provides perfect results for magazine, catalog and advertising printing.

Our MWC paper, which offers sustainable solutions with environmentally friendly production processes, meets the highest standards for professional printing applications.
