NSSC Paper

MEP Ambalaj NSSC paper is a paper type with high durability specially designed for packaging applications, produced with semi-chemical sulfate technology. It offers ideal solutions for packaging applications with its high durability and special design features.

Our NSSC paper provides excellent durability thanks to semi-chemical sulfate technology while delivering professional results with its high quality. With these features, it provides perfect results especially for packaging, box and applications requiring high durability.

With our sustainable production approach, we provide environmentally friendly NSSC paper solutions and supply the most suitable products for our customers' packaging needs.
