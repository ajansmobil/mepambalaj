NSSC Paper

Hatem Paper NSSC paper is a paper type with high durability produced with semi-chemical sulfate technology. It offers ideal solutions for packaging applications with its high durability and special design features.

Our NSSC paper provides excellent durability thanks to its semi-chemical sulfate structure while delivering professional results with its high quality. With these features, it provides perfect results especially for packaging, box and applications requiring durability.

With our sustainable production approach, we provide environmentally friendly NSSC paper solutions and supply the most suitable products for our customers' durability needs.
