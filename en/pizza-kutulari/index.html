Pizza Boxes

As MEP Packaging, we produce specially designed pizza boxes for delivery and restaurants. Our pizza boxes made from corrugated cardboard material offer ideal solutions for pizza packaging with their oil and moisture absorbing features.

With its durable and lightweight structure, it preserves the freshness of your pizzas during transportation. We produce solutions suitable for your brand's needs in different sizes and special printing options.

We offer environmentally friendly packaging solutions with sustainable material use.
