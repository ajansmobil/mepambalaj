SC Paper

Hatem Paper SC paper is a high-quality paper type produced with super calendered technology. It offers ideal solutions for magazine and catalog printing with high brightness and excellent print quality.

Our SC paper shows superior features in terms of surface smoothness and brightness thanks to the special calendering process. This way, perfect results are achieved in color printing and visual quality increases.

Our SC paper, designed for professional printing applications, is available in high grammage options and various sizes.
