Grease Proof Paper

Hatem Paper grease proof paper is a paper type specially designed for food packaging, produced with oil and moisture barrier properties and hygienic production processes. It offers ideal solutions for food packaging with its oil and moisture barrier features.

Our grease proof paper provides excellent protection thanks to its special barrier technology while delivering safe results with hygienic production processes. With these features, it provides perfect results especially for food, packaging and applications requiring protection.

With our sustainable production approach, we provide environmentally friendly grease proof paper solutions and supply the most suitable products for our customers' food packaging needs.
