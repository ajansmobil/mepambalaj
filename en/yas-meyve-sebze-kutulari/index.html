Fresh Fruit and Vegetable Boxes

As MEP Packaging, we produce special design packaging solutions for fresh fruit and vegetable products. With our packaging that absorbs all surface activity of microorganisms thanks to its porous structure, we extend the shelf life of your products.

We offer environmentally friendly packaging solutions using sustainable materials under hygienic production conditions. We produce solutions suitable for your brand's needs in different sizes and special design options.

We use packaging technologies that preserve the freshness and quality of your products.
